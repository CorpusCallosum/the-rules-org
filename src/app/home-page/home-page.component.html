Our Story

Our world is in crisis. It’s political, economical, environmental, and spiritual.

Everyone is asking why but few people are satisfied with the answers their politicians, the ‘experts’ or the media are giving them.

At The Rules, we work to make sense of these crises, and reveal a global malaise, experienced differently in local contexts.

Our work is to connect the dots: to show how these seemingly disparate issues are all interconnected. We reveal and critique the rules of our system but we also shine a light on a diversity of beautiful alternatives, showing that we have the power to change those rules.

As the Indian writer Arundhati Roy says: “Another world is not only possible, she is on her way. On a quiet day, I can hear her breathing.” The Rules exists to contribute to the transition to this more just and thriving world.
